Welcome to GNRT_AngularMeteor

AngularMeteor is an awesome way of building full-stack, real-time, reactive and mobile ready apps. http://www.angular-meteor.com

I love working with it, and as such have created a generator for some of the recursive tasks which I encounter.

The templates are currently based on Angular 2. I will be creating templates for AngularJS as well.

The project will evolve and mature, at this point it's pretty basic, but at the same time very useful.

Enjoy and feel free to contribute.

Lastly, thanks to the Meteor, Angular and AngularMeteor teams. AWESOME WORK!
